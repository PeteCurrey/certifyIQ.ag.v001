SECTION: What is Air Tightness Testing?

  Body:

    "Air tightness testing ГўВҖВ” also known as air permeability or pressure testing ГўВҖВ”

    measures how much air leaks through gaps and cracks in a building's envelope.

    The result is your building's air permeability rating, expressed in

    mГӮВі/h/mГӮВІ @ 50Pa (cubic metres of air leaking per square metre of building

Free tool for developers, self-builders and architects.



Input form (single-page, 10 questions):

  Q1: Property type (house / flat / bungalow / conversion)

  Q2: Total floor area (sqm)

  Q3: Number of storeys

  Q4: Wall U-value (numeric, with guide: "typical cavity = 0.18, good = 0.15")

  Q5: Roof U-value (numeric, guide: "270mm mineral wool = ~0.13")

  Q6: Floor U-value (numeric)

  Q7: Window U-value (guide: "modern double = 1.4, triple = 0.8")

  Q8: Heating system type (same picker as other tools)

  Q9: Heating fuel type

  Q10: Any renewables? (checklist: solar PV / solar thermal / heat pump / none)

  Air permeability design target (numeric, default: 5.0)



POST to /api/ai/sap-checker

  System prompt:

    "You are a qualified UK OCDEA (On-Construction Domestic Energy Assessor)

    with expertise in SAP 10.2 calculations and Part L1A Building Regulations.

    Based on the construction specification provided, estimate:

    1. Predicted DER (Dwelling Emission Rate) vs typical TER range

    2. Predicted DFEE vs typical TFEE range

    3. Likely compliance status: PASS | FAIL | MARGINAL

    4. If marginal or fail: the single most cost-effective change to achieve

       a pass, with estimated cost

    5. Estimated final EPC band

    6. SAP score estimate

    Return ONLY JSON: { der_estimate, ter_typical_range, dfee_estimate,

    tfee_typical_range, compliance_status, compliance_note, recommended_change,

    recommended_change_cost_estimate, epc_band, sap_score_estimate,

    confidence: 'indicative_only' }"



Results display:

  Big PASS / FAIL / MARGINAL badge (green/red/amber)

  DER vs TER comparison bar chart

  DFEE vs TFEE comparison bar chart

  EPC band estimate

  If fail/marginal: improvement recommendation card

  Disclaimer: "Indicative only. Actual SAP calculations must be carried

  out by an accredited OCDEA. Book a design stage SAP to get exact figures."

  Lead capture: "Get an exact SAP calculation ГўВҖВ” from ГӮВЈ195"

  Email field: "Email me these results" ГўВҶВ’ creates SAP lead
